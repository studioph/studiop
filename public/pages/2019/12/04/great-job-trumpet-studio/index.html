Great job Trumpet Studio!

December 4, 2019July 3, 2020  trumpet

I’ve been busy this week, back to back recitals! Last night was the Trumpet Studio recital, where we each performed a work by a French composer as part of our theme. There was phenomenal playing all around and I can’t wait for the next studio recital in the spring!

I’m so fortunate and thankful to be a part of this new family; I look forward to making more great music with everyone!

Thank you for a great recital!
