Philosophy

We carry melodies with us, singing and humming, long after the initial music has faded. Like a seed that grows into a tree, the metamorphosis of thematic material is a central focus of my work. I admire the melodic masters of the past and present, and strive to create melodies that will endure for future generations, both for moving image and the concert stage.
